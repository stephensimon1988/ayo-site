The Basics

AYÓ is a Filipino-inspired, ready-to-drink tea crafted with real ingredients. Inspired by the Philippines and crafted in Los Angeles, it’s a premium iced tea made for moments worth savoring — launching with two flavors, Lemongrass Black Tea and Pandan Green Tea.

It’s the feeling of welcome — a hand extended, a seat saved, a glass poured. That spirit of hospitality is the whole idea behind the brand: a hello in every sip.

Two to start: Lemongrass Black Tea (bright, citrusy, and bold) and Pandan Green Tea (clean, aromatic, and lightly sweet). Explore both on The Flavors page.

Ingredients & Taste

Real brewed tea, calamansi, a touch of honey, and natural flavors like lemongrass or pandan. No artificial ingredients, and Non-GMO. Each can is 12 FL OZ (355 mL).
