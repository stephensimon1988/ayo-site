FAQ

Good questions, friend.

What does “AYÓ” mean?

AYÓ is a warm Visayan greeting — the hello you call out when arriving at a friend’s home. Every can is meant to carry that same spirit of welcome.

When does Batch 001 launch?

Summer 2026, in limited quantities. Waitlist members get early access before the public release.

What’s inside the cans?

Real brewed tea, real fruit and botanicals — calamansi, lemongrass, pandan, and honey. Naturally sweetened, no artificial ingredients, non-GMO.

Where do you ship?

Batch 001 ships anywhere in the continental United States from our home base in Los Angeles.
